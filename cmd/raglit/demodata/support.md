# Support & SLAs

Priority-one incidents (full outage) have a 15-minute response target and a
4-hour resolution target. Priority-two (degraded) responds within one hour.

Support hours are 24/7 for enterprise, business hours for standard. Escalation
pages the on-call engineer through the incident tool after the response window
lapses.

Status is published at the public status page; subscribers get email on any
priority-one incident.
